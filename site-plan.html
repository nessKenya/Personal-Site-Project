Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat, Verdana, Open Sans

I am making a portfolio. Montserrat is quite readable and eye catching for those visiting the site to easily see it standing out. I have Verdana and Open Sans as the fall back fonts in the event that the browser doesn't support the Google fonts in use.

Paragraph Font: Roboto, Raleway, Helvetica, Sans-Serif

Roboto is good for paragraphs as it is well spaced out, readable and has good line height naturally. It makes the content under individual headlines to stand out for a portfolio. I have also web safe fonts for fall back.
